Budget-Friendly

At Durano Eco Farm, you can enjoy an enjoyable vacation for a very little cost. You won't need to worry too much because you can be certain that your money will be worth it.

Relaxing

You probably already know that free-flowing spring water is extremely clear, immaculate, and chilly enough to refresh you. This resort is one of many in Cebu that provides such great services.

Eco-Friendly

Durano Eco Farm Resort is an eco-farm in establishment that provides a variety of natural environments and wildlife that are being preserved and planted with different collections of plants and trees.

ABOUT

The tranquil Durano Eco Farm and Spring Resort is located in Carmen, Cebu. Our eight multi-level pools, which contain spring water from a nearby natural spring, provide cool relaxation amidst breathtaking panoramic views of the natural world. We provide cozy lodgings at reasonable prices, including cottages, treehouses, and cabins built for maximum comfort and enjoyment. Experience the peace and quiet at Durano Eco Farm and Spring Resort.
